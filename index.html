A COMPLETELY DIFFERENT COFFEE

Roasting is one of the most delicate and important moments in coffee production, as, thanks to it, the bean releases all the aromas and becomes ready for grinding. The artisanal one, unlike the industrial one, is more uniform and qualitatively more valuable.The beans turn brown releasing all their aroma, they increase in volume and the amount of caffeine is self-regulating.
